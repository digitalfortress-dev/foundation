# Leave & Absence Guidelines

## Overview

Leave requests must always go through the proper process to ensure smooth team operations and fair tracking.
Rest days are meant for you to truly recharge - planning ahead means you can fully switch off without worrying about work.


---
## Leave Types & Notice Requirements

| **Leave Type** | **Definition** | **Minimum Notice** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Standard Leave - Short-Term | Planned leave of 1-2 working days | 3 working days |
| Standard Leave - Long-Term | Planned leave of 3 or more working days | 8 working days (before sprint planning of affected sprint) |
| Urgent Leave | Unplanned leave with less than 5 working days notice | As soon as possible - SM / Team Lead must be informed immediately |
| Medical Leave | Leave due to illness or medical condition | Inform SM / Team Lead on Day 1. Submit medical certificate or prescription to HR within 2 working days of returning |

### Notes on Medical Leave

A valid **medical certificate or official prescription** from a licensed doctor or hospital is required.
Absence without proof will be treated as unapproved urgent leave.

---

## Leave Request Approval Process

### Step 1: Inform Your SM / Team Lead

For **all leave types**, SM / Team Lead must be informed **before or immediately upon** any absence - no exceptions, including urgent and emergency cases.

- **Developer / Project Member:** Inform your SM, Team Lead, or Manager and include a task handover plan before your absence.
- **SM / Team Lead / Manager:** Submit your request to Mr. Quynh (CEO) for approval.

### Step 2: Evaluation & Approval

SM / Team Lead assesses the sprint workload, team capacity, and coverage before approving.

#### Escalation to Board of Directors (BOD):

The following cases must be escalated to BOD before final approval:

- Long-term leave (≥ 3 days)
- Family emergencies
- Private matters
- Enrolling in a long-term course

### Step 3: Submit in DF App

After SM / Team Lead approval, **submit your leave in the DF App** for Finance and HR tracking.
This step is **mandatory for all leave types**, including urgent cases.

### Step 4: Final Approval & Notification

- SM / Team Lead / CEO grants final approval via email, Slack, or SMS.
- The team member on leave notifies all relevant members via the company Slack group including date, time, and reason.
- CC Mr. Quynh (CEO) & Ms. Phuong (Accountant) for tracking.

## Leave Request Format

All leave requests should follow this format:

> Hi [@Your team members], cc @Quynh Nguyen @Phuong Vo
>
> **- Time off:** [Start Time] - [End Time], [Date]
> **- Reason:** [Clear and specific reason]
> **- Handover:** [Person responsible for your tasks during absence]

**Example:**

> Hi @team, cc @Quynh Nguyen @Phuong Vo
>
> **- Time off:** All day, 05/05/2025
> **- Reason:** Medical appointment at City International Hospital
> **- Handover:** @Tai Le will cover my sprint tasks

---

## Task Handover Requirement

A task handover plan is required for all **Standard** and **Urgent** leave requests.
Requests submitted **without a handover plan may be rejected** by SM / Team Lead.

Ensure the following before your absence:

- Identify which tasks are in progress
- Assign each task to an appropriate team member
- Brief the person taking over on context and next steps

---

## Guidelines for Providing Reasons

To foster **transparency** and **team empathy**, all leave requests should include a clear and specific reason.

🚫 **Avoid vague terms like:**

- "Personal issues"
- "Private matters"
- "Busy with something"

✅ **Instead, be transparent and professional:**

- "Doctor's appointment at [clinic/hospital name]"
- "Attending a family wedding in [location]"
- "Handling paperwork at [government office name]"
- "Taking care of a sick family member"

---

## Warning & Compliance

Repeated failure to follow the leave process will result in the following actions:

| **Offence** | **Action** |
| --- | --- |
| 1st offence - no notice or missing DF App submission | Verbal warning from SM / Team Lead |
| 2nd offence - repeat within same year | Written warning from HR |
| 3rd offence - continued non-compliance | Escalation to Director for formal review |
| Severe - fraudulent medical certificate or job abandonment | Immediate disciplinary action up to termination |

Unapproved absences will be treated as **unpaid leave**
---

By following these guidelines, we create a supportive, accountable, and high-performing team environment for everyone.
